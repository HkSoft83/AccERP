 1. Liquidity Ratios (Short-term financial health)
Current Ratio = Current Assets / Current Liabilities

Quick Ratio (Acid-Test) = (Current Assets – Inventory) / Current Liabilities

Cash Ratio = Cash & Cash Equivalents / Current Liabilities

💰 2. Profitability Ratios (How well the business generates profit)
Gross Profit Margin = Gross Profit / Revenue

Net Profit Margin = Net Profit / Revenue

Operating Profit Margin = Operating Profit / Revenue

Return on Assets (ROA) = Net Profit / Total Assets

Return on Equity (ROE) = Net Profit / Shareholder’s Equity

⚙️ 3. Efficiency Ratios (How efficiently resources are used)
Inventory Turnover Ratio = Cost of Goods Sold / Average Inventory

Accounts Receivable Turnover = Net Credit Sales / Average A/R

Accounts Payable Turnover = Cost of Goods Sold / Average A/P

Asset Turnover Ratio = Revenue / Average Total Assets

📈 4. Solvency Ratios (Long-term financial stability)
Debt-to-Equity Ratio = Total Liabilities / Shareholder’s Equity

Debt Ratio = Total Liabilities / Total Assets

Interest Coverage Ratio = EBIT / Interest Expense

🕒 5. Activity / Working Capital Ratios
Working Capital = Current Assets – Current Liabilities

Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) = (A/R / Total Credit Sales) × No. of Days

Days Payable Outstanding (DPO) = (A/P / Cost of Sales) × No. of Days

Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC) = DSO + Inventory Days – DPO

Bonus (if public company):
Earnings Per Share (EPS) = Net Income / No. of Outstanding Shares

Price to Earnings Ratio (P/E) = Share Price / EPS
